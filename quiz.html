Easy questions:

Q1

In which city does the Colorado Avalanche play their home games?

Answer

Denver

Q2

What was the original name of the franchise before they moved to Colorado in 1995?

Answer

Quebec Nordiques

Q3

Who is the current captain of the Colorado Avalanche?

Answer

Gabriel Landeskog

Medium questions:

Q4

How many Stanley Cup championships has the franchise won in total?

Answer

Three (1996, 2001, and 2022)

Q5

Which legendary Czech forward, known for his playmaking, spent the majority of his career with the Avalanche and has his #23 jersey retired?

Answer

Milan Hejduk

Q6

Who was the head coach when the Avalanche won their most recent Stanley Cup in 2022?

Answer

Jared Bednar

Hard questions:

Q7

Which goaltender holds the franchise record for the most wins in a single regular season?

Answer

Semyon Varlamov (with 41 wins in the 2013-14 season)

Q8

Against which team did the Colorado Avalanche play in their first-ever Stanley Cup Finals appearance in 1996?

Answer

Florida Panthers

Q9

Who was the first player in Avalanche history to win the Conn Smythe Trophy (Playoff MVP)?

Answer

Joe Sakic (in 1996)
